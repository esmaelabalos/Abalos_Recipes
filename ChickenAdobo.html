Chicken Adobo Recipe

Preparation time: 20 mins Cook time: 25 mins Servings: 4-6 servings

Ingredients:

  • Chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and peppercorns.
  2. Add chicken thighs to the marinade and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© onions until translucent.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  5. Pour in the marinade, add bay leaves, and bring to a boil.
  6. Lower heat, cover, and simmer for 30-40 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Notes:

Adobo is traditionally served with steamed rice, which helps to soak up the flavorful sauce. You can also enjoy it with bread or noodles if you prefer.
