Pork Sisig Recipe

Preparation time: 20 mins Cook time: 30 mins Servings: 4-6 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork belly, boiled and chopped
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3-4 pieces green chili peppers, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons calamansi juice or lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Boil pork belly until tender, then chop it into small pieces.
  2. Mince the onion and garlic. Chop the green chili peppers.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced onion and garlic. Saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add chopped pork belly to the pan. Cook until slightly crispy.
  5. Pour soy sauce and calamansi juice into the pan. Mix well.
  6. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste preference.
  7. Add chopped green chili peppers to the mixture. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy with steamed rice.

Notes:

Once the pork is cooked and crispy, chop it into small pieces. The smaller the pieces, the better the texture of the sisig.
