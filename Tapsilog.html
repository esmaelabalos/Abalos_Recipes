Tapsilog Recipe

Preparation time: 15 mins
Cook time: 15 mins
Servings: 4 serving

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) beef sirloin or flank steak, thinly sliced against the grain
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper and cooking oil, for frying
  • 4 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced, Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the thinly sliced beef in minced garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, brown sugar, and black pepper for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  2. Heat cooking oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the marinated beef slices until browned and cooked through. Set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if needed and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and stir-fry until heated through and coated with the garlic and oil. Season with salt to taste.
  5. Fry eggs according to preference (usually sunny-side-up or over easy).
  6. Serve the beef tapa with garlic fried rice and a fried egg on the side.

Notes:

Tapsilog is best served hot and fresh. Assemble the dish just before serving to ensure that everything is warm and delicious.
