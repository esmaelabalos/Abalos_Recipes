Crispy Pata Recipe

Preparation time: 20 mins Cook time: 25 mins Servings: 4-6 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole pork leg (pata), about 2-3 kilograms
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 4 pieces bay leaves
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon whole peppercorns
  • Water for boiling
  • Salt for seasoning
  • Cooking oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Clean the pork leg and pat it dry with paper towels.
  2. Place the pork leg in a pot and cover it with water. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Boil the pork leg until tender, about 1.5 to 2 hours.
  4. Remove the pork leg from the pot and let it cool.
  5. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot.
  6. Carefully lower the pork leg into the hot oil and fry until the skin is crispy and golden brown, about 15-20 minutes.
  7. Remove the crispy pata from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  8. Serve hot with dipping sauce or vinegar on the side.

Notes:

Crispy pata is best enjoyed hot and fresh out of the fryer. Serve it with steamed rice and your favorite side dishes for a delicious and satisfying meal.
